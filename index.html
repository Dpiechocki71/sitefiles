About Us

The European Model Search & Image Agency, under the direction of Jason Dixon, Talent Agent & CEO, is a fully licensed and bonded boutique talent agency in the Tampa Bay, Florida area.  Although our main office is in central Pinellas County in Florida, we book people for jobs in other Florida markets, as well as nationally and internationally.  Our agency serves men, women, boys, and girls who have a passion for the industry and helps them achieve success in the acting and modeling genres of entertainment.   Jason’s own professional career experience as a model and actor spans over 20 years and is the main reason why many talented people aspiring to have a career as a model and/or actor, come to Jason for representation.

Our mission at European Model Search & Image is to empower and represent talented people who are pursuing a career in the entertainment industry and helping them reach their goals and dreams.  As a boutique agency, we are like a big family and we consider your success to be our success!  We never charge a fee.  We get paid when you get paid.

We welcome you to visit our talent submission page and submit your photo and information to be considered for representation.  Please allow up to 48-72 hours for a reply once you’ve submitted as we do receive a lot of applicants.
